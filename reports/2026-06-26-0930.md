### "invalid signature length" — mis-diagnosis corrected
- First blamed sx.js `getRSVFromSig` (65-byte vs Ledger 64-byte EIP-2098). After Wan posted the full error object: it's actually **MetaMask `KeyringController.signTypedMessage`** throwing before sx.js sees anything (Ledger `UNKNOWN_ERROR 0x6a00`).
- Conclusion: blind signing can't reliably fix EthSig; **EthTx (#2183) is the real fix**.

### EthTx votes not landing / mana relayer

- Root cause: the UI's **fire-and-forget `registerTransaction` POST to mana is lost**, so no `registered_transactions` row → relayer never submits L2 `authenticate`. Commit is orphaned but valid/permanent.
- Opened **issue #2186** (snapshot-labs/sx-monorepo): mana self-register from `CommitAdded`/`_commits`, or UI persist+retry.
- Three users hit this. Re-triggering is blocked (commit hash needs original `metadataUri`/reason, not recoverable from chain).
- Relayer architecture learned: EthSig/EthTx/StarkTx share the **same per-space mana relayer** (STRK-funded). EthTx fee split: user pays L1 in ETH, relayer pays L2 in STRK → no extra STRK burden from enabling EthTx.
